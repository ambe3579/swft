Google Search

What is SEO?

Search engine optimization (SEO) are techniques and tactics that improves your website visibility on the internet by making the website rank higher when user type certain relevant queries into search engines.

Why do you need SEO?

SEO connects your potential customers with the services you provide.
People type what they want into search engines to find what they want. The search engines shows them a list of what they may want. Getting ranked higher in that list means potential customers are more likely to see and click on your website.

Facebook Marketing

What is Facebook Marketing?

Facebook Marketing are techniques and tactics that matches your facebook page with facebook users that are showed interest in the service you are providing. You can then link them to your website from your facebook page.

Why do you need Facebook Marketing?

Facebook Marketing connects your potential customers with the services you provide.
Facebook is the most popular social network in the world. Facebook users upload, share and like to express their interests in certain topics. Facebook's algorithms target your promoted facebook page to users that expressed related interests.
